WELCOME!


Our practice serves the needs of individuals ages four to adult. While we specialize in providing unique services such as social skills groups and life skills programming for individuals on the Autism Spectrum, we also provide individual and family therapy for individuals with high anxiety, ADD/ADHD, and nonverbal learning disorder. Navigating a diagnosis can be overwhelming for a parent or caregiver. Wherever you are on your personal or family journey, it is our pleasure to join you and help you to reach the goals that you have set.
